About Us

Discover Nexads

Nexads Group’s story is one of vision, determination, and extraordinary growth. Our journey began with our CEO, who started as a freelancer providing marketing and social media services to a handful of clients. Fueled by ambition, he laid the foundation for what would become Nexads Consulting, unsure at first of how to navigate the path ahead. Determined to offer the highest quality services, he forged collaborations with talented professionals and took on the role of project director, orchestrating projects with precision and strategic insight.

What followed was nothing short of remarkable. From those humble beginnings, our CEO’s leadership propelled Nexads to the forefront, culminating in leading the most renowned political campaign of 2024—a testament to the trust and expertise we have earned. Our client base expanded rapidly, evolving from local partnerships to a prestigious roster that spans companies and industries around the globe.

Today, Nexads Group stands as a beacon of innovation and excellence, blending creative solutions with strategic precision. Our story is a testament to resilience, vision, and the power of collaboration, continuously inspiring us to empower businesses with services that resonate, engage, and drive impactful results.

CEO & Founder

Kyriakos Georgiou

Kyriakos Georgiou is the visionary Founder and CEO of Nexads Consulting, established in 2020. Nexads combines strategic business consulting and cutting-edge marketing solutions to help businesses grow and thrive.

With years of experience in strategic leadership and marketing, Kyriakos has a proven track record of helping businesses optimize operations and boost their market presence.
