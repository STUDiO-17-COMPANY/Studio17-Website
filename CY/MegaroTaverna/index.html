Est. 1992

More than just food,
it’s Filoxenia.

Nestled in the winding cobblestone streets of Limassol’s historic Old Town, Megaro is housed in a restored 19th-century stone mansion. We believe that Cypriot cuisine is best served the way our grandmothers made it: slow-cooked, full of flavor, and shared generously.

Whether you are a local looking for the comfort of home or a traveler seeking authenticity, our table is yours.
