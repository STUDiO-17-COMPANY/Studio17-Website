Our Philosophy

More than food.
It's about connection.

At 100PRATOS, we believe the best conversations happen over shared plates. Based in the heart of Setúbal, we source the freshest local seafood, artisanal cheeses, and sun-ripened produce to craft grazing tables and hot petiscos that spark joy.

The Hands Behind the Flavor

Meet Chef Rita Braz

Born from a passion for authentic flavors and the joy of hosting, Rita Braz founded 100PRATOS to bring the warmth of a Portuguese family table to your events.

With a deep respect for seasonal ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations, Rita transforms simple, fresh components into sophisticated Petiscos that encourage sharing, storytelling, and celebration.

"Cooking is my way of giving love."
Rita Braz
